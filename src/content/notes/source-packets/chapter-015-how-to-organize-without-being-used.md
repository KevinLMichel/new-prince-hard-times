## Accepted Source Lanes

- USAGov state and local government directories: https://www.usa.gov/state-local-governments
- FOIA and state/local public-records resources: https://www.usa.gov/foia
- Local public meeting agendas, minutes, budgets, contracts, ordinances, and board materials for any named campaign.
- Legal aid and civic organizations where specific organizing rights or risks are discussed.

## Risk Notes

- Keep organizing lawful, nonviolent, and accountable.
- Do not imply legal rights or meeting rules are uniform across jurisdictions.
- Any named campaign, official, or local dispute needs direct public records.
