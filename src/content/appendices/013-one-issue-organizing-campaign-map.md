Do not begin with everything.

## Campaign map

| Question | Answer |
| --- | --- |
| What exactly must change? | &nbsp; |
| Who can change it? | &nbsp; |
| What evidence do we have? | &nbsp; |
| What public deadline matters? | &nbsp; |
| Who is affected? | &nbsp; |
| Who can help? | &nbsp; |
| What would count as a first win? | &nbsp; |
| What could go wrong if we move too fast? | &nbsp; |
| What promise will we not make? | &nbsp; |
| Who records decisions after each meeting? | &nbsp; |

## Roles

Records, outreach, fact-checking, speaker, treasurer, de-escalator, meeting tracker, and follow-up lead.

## First move

Write the issue in one sentence a stranger can understand. If you cannot do that yet, keep studying.
