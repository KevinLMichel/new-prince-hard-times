When urgency rises, pause.

## Ask before acting

| Question | Answer before acting |
| --- | --- |
| Who profits from my urgency? | &nbsp; |
| What written proof exists? | &nbsp; |
| Can I verify this outside the seller's own materials? | &nbsp; |
| What happens if I wait 24 hours? | &nbsp; |
| What fees, data access, contract terms, or obligations are hidden? | &nbsp; |
| Would I tell someone I love to do this? | &nbsp; |
| Who can give me a calm second opinion? | &nbsp; |

## Warning signs

Decide now. Tell no one. Guaranteed. Secret method. Everyone else is lying. You are special. This will fix everything.

## First rule

If the offer cannot survive daylight, sleep, and one calm witness, it is not rescue.
