## Accepted Source Lanes

- Academic research on moral panic, political communication, misinformation, and media incentives.
- Political violence, violence-prevention, and democratic-resilience research for the "revolt without rot" framing.
- Platform transparency reports and advertising business disclosures where relevant.
- Credible journalism for event examples, used only with dates and context.

## Volatile Claims To Re-Check

- Named platform, campaign, influencer, or media claims.
- Any named unlawful/violent event or accused person.

## Risk Notes

- High rhetorical risk. Avoid accusing identifiable people of bad faith unless their public conduct/source language supports it.
- Public prose should not name, valorize, or recount an active accused-person case. The current chapter treats pressure, sympathy, and revolt abstractly, rejects violence, and redirects rage toward lawful organized power.
