## Protect first

Do not cut medicine, transportation to work, insurance, child essentials, needed maintenance, or food quality blindly. Some cuts create larger damage later.

## First move

Circle the three categories that moved most. Then ask whether the answer is household adjustment, income pressure, public accountability, or all three.
