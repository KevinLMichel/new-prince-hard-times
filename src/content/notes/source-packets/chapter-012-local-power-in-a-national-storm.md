## Accepted Source Lanes

- Local budgets, council minutes, school-board agendas, zoning codes, and public records for any named local case study.
- USAGov state and local government directory: https://www.usa.gov/state-local-governments
- Census local demographic and housing data: https://www.census.gov/
- HUD and local housing resources for zoning/housing claims: https://www.huduser.gov/portal/
- Local development agreements, tax-incentive records, utility filings, community-benefits agreements, and planning-board materials for public-return claims.
