## Ask for

- Itemized bill.
- Written reason for denial.
- Appeal deadline.
- Financial-assistance policy.
- Correct insurance information.
- Doctor or provider documentation if needed.

## First move

Make the call before the deadline. Anger may be deserved. Records win more often than volume.
