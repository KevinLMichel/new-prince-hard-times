Public comment is not a performance. It is a record.

## Template

My name is [name], and I live/work in [place].

I am speaking about [specific agenda item or issue].

The problem is [one or two sentences].

The evidence is [bill, date, incident, budget line, policy, public record, or direct experience].

The requested action is [specific vote, delay, amendment, audit, meeting, record release, or policy change].

Please state publicly who is responsible for the next step and when residents will receive an answer.

## Rule

Be specific enough that silence becomes visible.
