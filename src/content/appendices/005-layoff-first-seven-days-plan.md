Job loss is an event. Do not let it become a verdict.

## Day 1

Read documents. Do not sign what you do not understand. Save contacts, benefits information, severance papers, and final pay details.

## Days 2-3

File for unemployment or relevant benefits if eligible. List cash, bills, deadlines, health coverage, and household risks. Tell trusted people directly that you are looking.

## Days 4-5

Update resume, references, proof of work, and a simple tracking sheet for applications and calls.

## Days 6-7

Apply steadily, not wildly. Set a daily rhythm: search, apply, contact people, move the body, handle one household task, and stop before panic turns the day into fog.

## First rule

Do not disappear from the people who know your work.
