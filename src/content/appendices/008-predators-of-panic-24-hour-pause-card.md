When urgency rises, pause.

## Ask before acting

- Who profits from my urgency?
- What written proof exists?
- Can I verify this outside the seller's own materials?
- What happens if I wait 24 hours?
- What fees, data access, contract terms, or obligations are hidden?
- Would I tell someone I love to do this?
- Who can give me a calm second opinion?

## Warning signs

Decide now. Tell no one. Guaranteed. Secret method. Everyone else is lying. You are special. This will fix everything.

## First rule

If the offer cannot survive daylight, sleep, and one calm witness, it is not rescue.
