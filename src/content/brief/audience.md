title: "Audience"

summary: "Who this is for"
---

This book is for people who feel squeezed but refuse to become bitter.

Workers facing layoffs. Parents crushed by prices. Young people priced out of adulthood. Veterans of broken promises. Small business owners caught between rising costs and exhausted customers. Teachers, nurses, tradespeople, students, organizers, and anyone who suspects the game is rigged but still wants to play well enough to change it.

It is not written for cynics.

It is written for fighters who still possess a conscience.
