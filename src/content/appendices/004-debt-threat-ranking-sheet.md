## Rank by danger

First protect shelter, utilities, transportation to work, legal safety, health, and income. High-interest debt matters, but not all late payments carry the same immediate consequence.

## Call script

"I am reviewing my account and need to understand my options before this becomes worse. Can you explain hardship plans, due-date changes, fee waivers, payment plans, or written dispute procedures?"

Write down the date, representative, reference number, and promised next step.
